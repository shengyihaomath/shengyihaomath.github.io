Welcome to my homepage. I am a Ph.D. student in Applied Mathematics at the University of Connecticut. My advisor is Professor George Yin.

My research interests lie in stochastic control, singular perturbations, averaging principles, weak convergence, forward-backward stochastic differential equations, and regime-switching diffusion systems.

Research

My research focuses on stochastic dynamical systems with multiple time scales. I am particularly interested in understanding how fast-scale randomness affects the effective behavior of slow systems, with applications to stochastic control, FBSDEs/BSDEs, and semilinear parabolic PDEs.
